Hello, I'm Gregorio Jaca. Welcome to my personal corner of the web. This site is a minimalist reflection of my digital identity and interests, specifically in the intersection of algorithms, physics, and distributed systems.

The background you see is a real-time simulation of percolation theory. Percolation is a mathematical framework that describes the behavior of connected clusters in a random graph or lattice. It has profound applications in material science, neurology, and phase transitions.

Currently, I am exploring the boundaries of agentic AI and how autonomous systems can be leveraged to solve complex engineering challenges. I find beauty in simplicity and robustness, often leaning towards minimal and efficient implementations in all my projects.

This portfolio is built using Astro and Tailwind CSS, prioritizing performance and clarity. It is statically generated and hosted on GitHub Pages, ensuring a fast and reliable experience for all visitors across different devices.
